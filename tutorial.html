Breadwinner!

Goal

You're a germ! You're hungry, but luckily you've found some bread. You've got competition though, avoid the other germs and capture at least 80% of the slice to progress to the next level! You have 2 minutes per level, and you start with 5 lives. Good luck!

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move, or WASD. You are the red diamond, the germs are the green circles. Don't let them touch you or your path! If you're on mobile, tough luck. This probably isn't the game for you.
